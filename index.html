    About Me

    Hello there, welcome.
    I am a guy who design websites and web apps, pretty straightforward. But believe me a lot of goes into arranging pixels.

    I have over 5 years of experience and I am still learning new things on a daily basis. Technology is constantly evolving, and stasis means mediocrity. I will be happy to help you with your next website or startup idea which needs web implementation.

    I’m specialized in

    • IA

      Any successful design starts with the organization of information. I create an information architecture (IA) that works.

    • UI

      The visual representation of information is part art part science. I mix visual design principles with cognitive to create UI.

    • UX

      UI is just one part of design equation, i.e. how it looks. UX is about how it actually works, which I design with great care.

    • IxD

      I work with animation designers to design the interactions and micro-interactions that enhance your website experience.

    • Branding

      I work with branding pros to design brand identity guidelines, logo, branding collaterals to ensure brand consistency.

    • Responsive

      The present and future of the internet is mobile. All of my designs are fully responsive and mobile-friendly.
